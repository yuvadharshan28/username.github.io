Who We Are

About Chiptrix

Born from a simple belief — everyone deserves access to great technology.

🎯

Our Mission

To bridge the digital divide by making quality refurbished electronics accessible and affordable for all. We believe technology shouldn't be a luxury — it should be a right.

👁️

Our Vision

A world where every student, professional, and family can access the technology they need without financial strain. Sustainability meets affordability.

💚

Our Values

Integrity in every repair, transparency in every price, and care in every interaction. We stand behind every device we refurbish with a comprehensive warranty.

📖

Our Story

Chiptrix started when our founders noticed how many perfectly good electronics were discarded simply because of minor issues. We saw an opportunity — restore these devices to peak performance and pass the savings on to people who need them most. Today, we're a growing startup turning e-waste into opportunity.
